Protein Pancakes

Homemade Protein Pancakes made with Kodiak Cakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Kodiak Cakes
  • Milk (as much as needed)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp of Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Pour Kodiak mix into a bowl and mix with milk until smooth.
  2. Crack egg into mix and stir until blended well.
  3. Add vanilla extract into mix and stir up if desired.
  4. Add scoop of mix into the pan and cook pancakes untilk golden brown
